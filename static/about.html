California Grown

I am a california native that spent the majority of my childhood playing with computers, tinkering with all my electronics, and experimenting with my father's expensive gadgets. I spent 10 years in the Air Force working as a cryptologist and a Korean Linguist, but the joy of developing and coding had gotten its hooks in early on. I had always been interested in web design, but I wanted to go a step further. I aim to be the the best front end develepor I can, with a major focus on elegance, future proofing, and lightweight code. At the same time, I think my experiences in the past have tought me that I cannot pigeon hole myself into one expertise, so I endeaver to have a breadth of experience in Back End development and server architecture in order to understand both sides even better.
